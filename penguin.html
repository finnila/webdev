Percy the Penguin

Percy is our playful Emperor penguin who came to us from Antarctica. Emperor penguins are the tallest and heaviest of all living penguin species. Percy stands about 4 feet tall and is an excellent swimmer.

In the wild, Emperor penguins can dive up to 1800 feet deep and hold their breath for up to 20 minutes! Percy loves to show off his swimming skills during feeding time and enjoys sliding on his belly during our daily penguin parades.
