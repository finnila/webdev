Emma the Elephant

Emma is our gentle African elephant who loves playing with water. African elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. Emma weighs around 7,000 pounds and continues to grow! She is incredibly intelligent and has learned to perform several behaviors that help our veterinarians check her health.

Elephants are known for their excellent memory and complex social relationships. Emma spends her days eating (up to 300 pounds of food per day!), playing with enrichment toys, and taking mud baths to keep cool.
