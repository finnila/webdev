Grace the Giraffe

Grace is our tallest resident, standing at an impressive 16 feet tall! She is a Masai giraffe, characterized by her distinctive pattern of dark brown, angular patches. Grace has been with us since she was young and has grown into a beautiful adult giraffe.

Giraffes like Grace have incredibly long tongues (up to 50 cm!) that help them reach leaves high up in trees. Their unique pattern of spots helps them camouflage in the wild. Grace enjoys eating acacia leaves and participating in our daily giraffe feeding sessions where visitors can feed her special giraffe treats.
